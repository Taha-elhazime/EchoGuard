2. Arduino R4 Wifi (or any microcontroller that has a 5v output): 30€

3. Jumper cables and breadboard(in case the pcb is not used):  11€

4. 2pcs HAXIHA A097 Metal Gear Torque Digital Servo Motor (40mm by 19mm): 14€

5. Trust Oran web camera (1080p 30fps): 20€

6. 3pcs small bearings: 7€

## Sensor:

2. ELEGOO ultrasonic sensor HC-SRO4: 10€ for 5 pcs

3. 1pcs GTIWUNG Rocker switch: 10€ for 35pcs
